Module 04 of 06 — Mum 180

The Trap You
Set for Yourself

Towards and away thinking. Why trying not to be a bad mum might be making you one.

Time: 30–40 min
Sections: 3
Section 1
Towards and Away

Mothers are either trying to move towards the idea of what they think a good mother is — or they're moving away from the opposite, where they've had a poor experience with their own mother, and now they're moving away from that.

The things you think about the most will express themselves in your thoughts, actions and behaviours.
Section 2
The Paradox

Primary and secondary goal setting, mixed with this towards and away thinking, creates a paradox where the focus is so much on not becoming the bad mother that you fall into the trap.

The things you think about the most are the poor behaviours. And if that's what you're focused on — unfortunately that starts to leak out.

And again, it creates that guilty loop and the selfish loop.
Reflection
Are you moving towards something or away from something? Be honest — what is the thing you are actually focused on most?
Section 3
Reflection
Reflection
Describe the mum you want to be — not the one you're afraid of becoming. The one you are choosing to be.
Reflection
What would change if you shifted from an away focus to a towards focus? What would you think about differently?
