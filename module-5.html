Section 01

Version of Self

You don't have one identity. You have a current version and an available version. The current version is shaped by your history, your habits, your evidence base. The available version is what you're actually capable of — the version that exists in your high-capacity state.

The Identity Problem: Most people try to change their behaviour while keeping the same identity. It doesn't work. Behaviour follows identity. Change who you believe you are — through accumulated evidence — and behaviour changes automatically.

Think Small, Start Horribly isn't about lowering your standards. It's about closing the gap between your current version and your available version through small, horrible, uncomfortable actions that generate real evidence of who you're becoming.
