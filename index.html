Questions

What You Need to Know

Is this a video course? +
No. This is a fully interactive web portal — think less "watch me talk" and more "do the work." Each module has reading, interactive diagnostics, reflection exercises, sliders, selection tools, and evidence logs. Everything saves automatically. You can export a PDF of your completed work at any time.
How long does it take to complete? +
Each module takes 45–60 minutes to complete honestly. The full programme is approximately 5–7 hours of real work. But the portal is built to be returned to — your answers update as you change, and the evidence log is designed to grow over time.
Does it work on mobile? +
Yes. The portal is a Progressive Web App — fully responsive and designed for mobile. You can also add it to your home screen for app-like access. All your progress saves locally on your device.
Where does my data go? +
Everything saves to your browser's local storage — it stays on your device. Your reflections, audit scores, and progress never leave your machine. This is by design: the work is yours.
What if I already know this stuff? +
Then you're in the Conceptual Landscape — and Module 03 is specifically about you. Knowing something and having actually applied it are two different things. The programme is built to surface that gap, not just add to your knowledge pile.
Is there a payment plan? +
Payment plans are available on the Circuit + Coaching tier. Contact hello@thinksmallstarthorribly.com to arrange. The Foundation Pack and Full Programme are single-payment only.
